What's in the box

Five things, one app.

Everything a regatta director already does — just done in one place, in a tenth of the time, with a record of every action.

01 · Entries

Data in. Regatta out.

Drop in your Entry system download, clubs, events, crews and competitors land in seconds. Re-upload anytime — race-day flags and sent invites survive.

  • Zip import in one click
  • Diff added/removed/modified on every re-upload
  • Coaches dedup’d across multiple clubs
02 · Communications

Six scopes. Two-way replies.

Reach one coach, one crew, one event, one club, the committee, or every coach you've got — with push first and email as the silent backup.

  • Six precise scopes, not "everyone"
  • Replies thread privately back to you
  • Confirmation step on global sends
03 · Dashboards

The screen you actually look at.

Clubs overview, doubling-up alerts, crew status, payment progress — the spreadsheets you used to chase, alive on a screen.

  • Doubling-up flagged automatically
  • Per-club entry and payment view
  • Mark crews eliminated on race day
04 · The print pack

Apps with the app.

Generate a print-ready boat number for time trials, with club, event and crew info. Create T-shirt name list, blade colours sheet, programme infographic and some social media posts.

  • Some insightful social media posts
  • T-shirt names for your shop
  • Infographic for socials, and website
  • Zero manual edits per new club
05 · Volunteers

Slots that fill themselves.

Create the shifts you need, share one signup link, watch capacity tick up. Each volunteer gets their own timetable for the day.

  • Self-serve signup, no logins
  • Per-volunteer personal timetable
  • Live capacity, no double-booking
How it works

Day one to race day, in three steps.

Most regatta directors set up Ludum in a single sitting. Here's the path from a fresh login to a coach's phone buzzing on the towpath.

01 · Import

Drop in the Entries Data.

Clubs, events, crews and competitors populate. Branding, dates and your sender email go in once. Your race-day eliminations and sent invites are preserved on every re-import.

02 · Invite

One click, every coach.

Bulk magic-link invites go out, deduped by email. Coaches install the app, see their crews, and you can start composing. Re-clicking only emails new coaches — never spam.

03 · Run the day

Broadcast, swap, mark, hand off.

Send the wind warning to every crew on the water. Swap a lane. Mark an elimination. Hand the printer the brochure pack. Watch read-receipts roll in.

Why Ludum

We built this because we run regattas.

Ludum started inside the National Schools' Regatta — one of the busiest three-day rowing events in the World, with hundreds of crews, dozens of clubs and a coach for every one of them. The communication tools didn't exist. So we built them.

The result is a platform that takes the messy reality of a competitive regatta — coaches who run for three clubs, last-minute draws, the wind picking up at the wrong end of the course, the printer who needs the blade brochure on Wednesday — and turns it into an inbox you can actually run an event from.

It's now generalising to any multi-club rowing event.

Audit logs on every action

Every import, every send, every edit — captured. Nothing happens silently.

Safety modal on global sends

The "everyone, everywhere" send is one click away — but never one click. A confirmation step keeps the wrong message from going wide.

Private replies, not Reply-All

Coaches reply to broadcasts privately. No noise for the other 311 people on the thread.
